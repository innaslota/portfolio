About Me

My introdution

I have always liked Information Technology (I won competitions and olympiads) and foreign languages at school. When I had to enter the university, I gave preference to languages, but the desire for computer science never left me.

Therefore, just after graduation, I found a job that matched my competencies in an IT company. I have been constantly improving my skills and in 2021 I ended up at Wix Answers team as an advanced Support Engineer. It was here that I started learning HTML, CSS and Java Script. I really liked programming and then I decided to develop in this direction.

Therefore, in 2022, I was studying on my own using Java Script textbooks, went through various bootcamps, did daily exercises and got into the SheCodes academy, where I successfully completed 4 courses.

Now I continue to study, develop my skills, learn new programming techniques and I hope to find my first job as a web developer in the nearest future.
